2. Place cut sweet potatoes on sheet pan and cover with foil. Place in freezer for at least 1 hour.
3. Adjust oven racks to lower middle position, place sheet pan into oven, and set oven to 300Â°F. Roast until a thin skewer inserted into potatoes meets no resistance, about 1 hour.
4. Remove foil, increase oven temperature to 425Â°F, and carefully flip potatoes with thin offset spatula. Roast until potatoes are browned and sugars begin to caramelize, about 30 minutes longer.
4. Transfer roasted potatoes to a large bowl. Toss with remaining olive oil and maple syrup *(or agave nectar, if using)*. Serve immediately.
